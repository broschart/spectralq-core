Censys Search

Source: Censys Search API v2

Searches for exposed hosts and services on the internet – with geographic filter (country, city) and port filter. "Live" executes the query and shows all found hosts with open ports, service and ASN. Requires a free Censys account (censys.io).

Create a Censys search to monitor exposed services in a specific country or region. Examples: location.country_code=DE and services.port=22
